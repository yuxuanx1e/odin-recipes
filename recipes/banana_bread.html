Banana Bread Recipe

World-famous Banana Bread. Easy to make!

This is recipe for making a world famous banana bread.

Ingredients

  • 4 bananas
  • 190g plain flour
  • 4 table spoons of vegetable oil
  • 150g sugar
  • Vanilla extract
  • Powder cinnamon

Recipe instructions

  1. Mash bananas
  2. Add plain flour
  3. Add 4 table spoons of vegetable oil
  4. Add 150g sugar
  5. Mix thoroughly
  6. Add Vanilla extract
  7. Add Powder cinnamon
  8. Bake in oven at 18- degrees celcius for 30 minutes
